    There are many kinds of debate, but the type I debate in is called Public Forum.
    Public Forum is a persuasive 2v2 style of debate, where opponents do not know what side they will be on until the beginning of the round.
    The Feburary PF deabte topic was about Urbanization in West Africa, and weighing the pros and cons.
    The Pro side is saying that Urbanization in West Africa is good, while the Con is opposing that.
    Here are some Pro arguments: 
    - Growth
    - Healthcare
    - Gender Equality
    - Infrastructure
    Here are some Con arguments:
    - Biodiversity
    - Food inequality
    - Water pollution
    - Urban poverty
    Here is the structure of a normal PF debate:
    Pro - Constructive - speaker A

    Con - Constructive - speaker A

    Cross-fire - asking questions - Both A speakers

    Pro - Rebuttal - speaker B

    Con - Rebuttal - speaker B

    Cross-fire - Both B speakers

    Pro - Summary - speaker A

    Con - Summary - speaker A

    Cross-fire - All speakers

    Pro - Final Focus - speaker B

    Con - Final Focus - speaker B
